Colophon

Complete Fiction
was compiled from short stories, novelettes, novellas and short novels written between 1897 and 1936 by
H. P. Lovecraft.

This ebook was produced for
Updike.org
by
Jared Updike,
and is based on the ebook produced by
Alex Cabal
for
Standard Ebooks,
and on transcriptions provided by
The H. P. Lovecraft Archive,
and on transcriptions produced between 2009 and 2015 by
Greg Weeks, Mary Meehan, David Starner, Stephen Blundell, and
The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg,
and on a transcription produced in 2019 by
Jason Bell
for the
Standard Ebooks project,
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive.

The cover was adapted from
The Ruins of Holyrood Chapel,
a painting completed around 1824 by
Louis Daguerre
and featured in
Google Arts & Culture.
The cover was created in
Affinity Designer
in 2020 by
Jared Updike.

The source repository for this ebook is available on
GitHub.
